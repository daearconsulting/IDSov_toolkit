## What the Nation Retains

Regardless of consent granted, the [Nation Name] Nation retains:

**Ownership:** All data collected about [Territory Name] is owned by
the [Nation Name] Nation, not by the research team or their institution.

**Control:** The Nation retains the right to:
- Review all findings before external publication
- Decline to authorize publication of specific findings
- Require modification of framing or conclusions
- Suspend or terminate data collection at any time

**Access:** The Nation has the right to access all data and findings
at any time, in usable formats, including after the project ends.

**Possession:** The Nation will receive a complete copy of all data
before this project concludes.

## Withdrawal of Consent

The [Nation Name] Nation may withdraw consent at any time without
penalty by providing written notice to [Research team contact].

Upon withdrawal:
1. All data collection ceases immediately
2. Analysis of existing data ceases unless specifically authorized
3. Data already collected is returned to the Nation
4. No additional publication or sharing occurs
